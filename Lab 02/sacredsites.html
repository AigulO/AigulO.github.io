Sacred places of Turkistan region

The Turkestan region has attracted travelers since ancient times with its cult and sacred places. Listed below are just a few of them:

  • Mausoleum of Arystan-baba
  • Mausoleum of Gaukhar-ana
  • Mausoleum of Ukasha-Ata
  • Domalak Ana Mausoleum (Holy Mother)
  • Mausoleum of Rabigi Sultan Begim
  • Hilvet Semi-Underground Mosque
  • Mausoleum of Gaukhar Ana
  • Minaret of the Prophet Khyzyr
  • Mausoleum of Ismail-ata
  • Nuh's ship
